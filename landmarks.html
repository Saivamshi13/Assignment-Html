Charminar Hyderabad, Telangana, India The Charminar is a monument located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It was constructed in 1591 and is a symbol of Hyderabad, officially incorporated in the emblem of Telangana. The monument is located in the heart of Hyderabad city on the east bank of Musi River on Charminar Road. It overlooks the popular Mecca Masjid on its southwest, while the lively Laad Bazaar lies on the western side of the monument
Ananthagiri Hills Vikarabad, Telangana, India Ananthagiri Hills is located in Vikarabad district, Telangana, India. The hills are the main water source for Osmansagar and Himayathsagar. It is considered as one of the dense forests in Telangana region. The hills are located about 10 km from Vikarabad
Taj Mahal Agra, India A white marble mausoleum built in the 17th century by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.
Eiffel Tower Paris, France The Eiffel Tower is located in Paris, France, the French capital and a beautiful city bisected by the Seine River. It is in the center of Paris, on the banks of the Seine, in the 7th arrondissement. It is on the Champs de Mars at 5 Avenue Anatole France, between the Trocadero and the fields of Mars. It is an iron tower named after the engineer Alexandar Gustave Eiffel, who designed and built it in 1889.
Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple Yadagirigutta, Telangana, India Sri Lakshminarasimha Swamy Temple or Yadagirigutta is a popular Hindu Temple of Narasimha Swamy, an incarnation Lord Vishnu. It is situated on a hillock in the Nalgonda district, Telangana, Yadagirigutta is at a distance of 6 KM from Riagir Railway station and at a distance of 13 KM from Bhongir Town and 60 kilometers from the city of Hyderabad.
