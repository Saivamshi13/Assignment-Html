The Flash (DC) 2023 Barry Allen is struck by a bolt of lightning and thus, an extraordinary power is born inside him: The Speed Force. When he uses this power to run back in time and save his mother, he creates a world without heroes and General Zod has returned. To defeat him, his only hope rests in the hands of a retired Batman, another Barry and an imprisoned kryptonian.
Bholaa 2023 Bhola is a movie that is a remake of the Tamil movie Kaithi (2019). The movie follows an ex-convict named Bholaa (Ajay Devgn) who desires to meet his daughter after a 10-year sentence. However, his plans are interrupted by a well-orchestrated drug bust conducted by a law enforcement agency. The story unfolds in a span of a night when Bholaa has to drive a truck full of poisoned cops to medical aid with a cook and an IPS officer Diana Joseph (Tabu) for company. The trio is chasing against time as a dreaded drug lord and his henchmen are out to kill Diana, who has seized a massive illegal consignment of drugs.
Tu Jhoothi Main Makkaar 2023 Tu Jhoothi Main Makkaar is a Bollywood film that explores the consequences of a clash between family and freedom, between casual fling and lifelong fidelity, between a girl who is done with living under the same roof with her extended brood and a boy for whom filial bonds matter as much as love does. The film follows the story of Mickey, a son of a rich family, who is involved in multiple businesses, including a "break-up" service in collaboration with his best friend Mannu Dabas. During Dabbas' bachelor's party in Spain, Mickey falls in love with Tinni, a practical and fun-loving girl, who belongs to the family of CAs. The film is described as a battle of wits in the world of romantic relationships
