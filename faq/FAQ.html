What is the role of son goku in dragon ball?

Son Goku is the main protagonist of the Dragon Ball series. He is a Saiyan raised on Earth who practices martial arts and possesses superhuman abilities. He goes on adventures to find the Dragon Balls, which can grant any wish, and to protect the Earth from various threats. He is also the husband of Chi-Chi, the father of Gohan and Goten, and the grandfather of Pan12. He is based on Sun Wukong, the Monkey King from the Chinese novel Journey to the West2.
